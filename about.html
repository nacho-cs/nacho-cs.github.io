About:

Why I created this website?

  • In the beginning, I created this website as a project to become more familiar with js and frontend development in general
  • However, people then started to actually use the website, so I actually had to maintain it
  • Keep in mind that I'm still a student, and I have schoolwork to do, so I'm not going to be constantly keeping the website up to date

My main goals

  • I want people to have as much information as they can about MCSR Ranked.
  • I want to give that information in a way that is easily accessible

How can I support you?

  • Please make sure to be descriptive
  • Other than that, you can spread the word about this
